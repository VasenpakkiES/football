Our Story

Welcome to Blooming Florals! Based in Helsinki, Finland, we have been spreading joy through the beauty of flowers for over a decade. Our shop prides itself on offering a diverse range of floral arrangements and plants to cater to all occasions, from weddings and birthdays to corporate events and everyday celebrations.

Our mission is to provide our customers with the freshest flowers, expertly arranged by our talented florists. We source our blooms from local growers as well as from around the world to ensure quality and variety. Our commitment to customer satisfaction and attention to detail has made us a beloved part of the Helsinki community.

Whether you are looking for a classic bouquet, a unique gift, or simply want to brighten up your home with beautiful plants, Blooming Florals is here to help. Thank you for choosing us to be a part of your special moments!

Meet the Team

Our team is passionate about flowers and dedicated to making every customer's experience exceptional. From our skilled florists to our friendly customer service team, each member plays a crucial role in delivering excellence.

Careers

We are always looking for enthusiastic individuals to join our team. If you have a passion for flowers and a love for customer service, consider applying for one of our open positions. Check out our careers page for current job openings and application instructions.
